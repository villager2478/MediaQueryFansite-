Survive From Drop to Endgame

Winning a match is all about good rotations, smart fights, and carrying the right gear. These tips focus on positioning and simple loadouts you can actually use.

Early Game

  • Land somewhere you know well, even if it’s not the most popular POI.
  • Grab at least one close-range and one mid-range weapon.
  • Carry early shields before worrying about snipers.

Mid Game

  • Upgrade your weapons when it’s safe.
  • Rotate with the storm instead of fighting in it.
  • Ignore Third-party fights that are already noisy and messy.

End Game

  • Hold high ground whenever possible.
  • Play with cover and build when needed.
  • Stay calm in final 1v1 and use heals smartly.

Recommended Settings

These are example settings newer players often try:

  • Graphic settings: use low graphic settings for best fps
  • Sensitivity: start low and increase slowly
  • Colorblind Mode: use the best colorblind mode to see in storm
